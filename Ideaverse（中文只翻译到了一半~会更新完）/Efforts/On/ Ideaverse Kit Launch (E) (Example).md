> ## ACE gives you 3 Spaces for the 3 Head Spaces of PKMACE 为 PKM 的 3 个顶部空间提供 3 个空间
> 
> In PKM, there are three main Head Spaces to move between.在 PKM 中，有三个主要的头部空间可以在它们之间移动。
> 
> - Knowledge-based (The Atlas folder)基于知识（阿特拉斯文件夹）
> - Time-based (The Calendar folder)基于时间（“日历”文件夹）
> - Action-based (The Efforts folder)基于操作（“工作”文件夹）
> 
> You may find yourself completely immersed in a single headspace (a single folder), while completely neglecting the other two. You may even start to question if you need the other two folders.您可能会发现自己完全沉浸在一个顶部空间（单个文件夹）中，而完全忽略了其他两个。您甚至可能开始质疑是否需要其他两个文件夹。
> 
> Don’t worry, _**life is long and the pendulum will swing.**_别担心，生命很长，钟摆会摆动
> 
> - You may be spending time in **Efforts** when...you find a new interest and wonder how to manage all the knowledge coming in. 你可能会花时间在努力中，当...你发现了一个新的兴趣，想知道如何管理所有的知识。
>     ​  
>     So it's time to shift to the **Atlas**.  所以是时候转向阿特拉斯了 
>     ​
> - You may be spending time in **Atlas** when...you start a new job and have to rapidly take a bunch of daily notes to make sense of later.  您可能会在阿特拉斯中度过一段时间，当...你开始一份新工作，必须迅速做一堆日常笔记，以便以后理解。
>     ​  
>     So it's time to shift to **Calendar**.  所以是时候转向日历了
>     ​
> - You may be spending time in **Calendar** when...you have a good sense of your commitments in your new job and are ready to start managing things.  
>     ​  您可能在日历中花费时间，当...你对新工作的承诺有很好的了解，并准备开始管理事情。

>     So it's time to shift back to **Efforts**.所以是时候回到努力了
> 
> See how this is working?看看这是如何工作的？

> ## ACE helps you with Context-SwitchingACE 可帮助您进行上下文切换
> 
> It's hard to switch contexts. It's also hard to know when you should. ACE helps with that.很难切换上下文。也很难知道什么时候应该这样做。ACE对此有所帮助。
> 
> When I’m frolicking in the forest of new information, it's my own enthusiasm that propels my learning.当我在新信息的森林中嬉戏时，是我自己的热情推动了我的学习。
> 
> Question: Where do you think I am spending my time then?  
> ​Answer: In the **Atlas**—where I'm note-making and sensemaking on things that may not be urgent, but still feel important.
> 问：那你认为我把时间花在哪里？
> 答：在地图集里——我正在对可能不紧急但仍然觉得重要的事情做笔记和理解。
> 
> However, there are long stretches of time where my frolic becomes a more of a march. I need to make progress on things that have a clear direction with tangible outcomes.然而，在很长一段时间里，我的嬉戏更像是一种进行曲。我需要在有明确方向和切实成果的事情上取得进展。
> 
> Question: Where do you think I spend my time then?  
> ​Answer: In **Efforts**—where I'm executing on things that are important and urgent.
> 问：那你觉得我把时间花在哪里？
> 答：在努力中——我正在执行重要和紧急的事情。
> 
> And there are times where I’m quickly taking meeting notes, or logging things I care about, or planning the month and I find myself spending time in daily, weekly, and monthly notes; freewriting, compiling, planning, and consolidating.
> 有时我会快速记会议笔记，或记录我关心的事情，或计划一个月，我发现自己花时间在每天、每周和每月的笔记上;自由写作、编译、规划和整合。
> 
> Question: Where do you think I spend my time then?  
> ​Answer: In **Calendar**—where I'm working with Past Me, Present Me, & Future Me.
> 问：那你觉得我把时间花在哪里？
> 答：在日历中，我正在使用 Past Me、Present Me 和 Future Me。
> ​
> 
> These context switches can happen in the moment, hourly, daily, weekly, and so on.这些上下文切换可以在当下、每小时、每天、每周等发生。
> 
> ACE honors Knowledge, Time, and Action equally—and naturally nudges you to remember to give any neglected Headspace some attention.ACE 同样尊重知识、时间和行动——并自然地促使您记住要关注任何被忽视的顶空。
