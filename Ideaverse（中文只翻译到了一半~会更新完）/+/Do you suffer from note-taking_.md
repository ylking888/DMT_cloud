## Examples 例子
Relatable Example相关示例

- When was the first time you heard the word "notes" into was probably with "notebook" (Show a notebook). You were told to take notes. Forced to, really. Not fun. Forget that. Replace it with something YOU care about. When you take notes on things you find interesting, the game changes. You feel empowered because you can more powerfully, wait for it...learn, remember, and create.你第一次听到“笔记”这个词是什么时候，大概是用“笔记本”（Show a notebook）说的。你被告知要做笔记。被迫，真的。不好玩。算了。用你关心的东西代替它。当你记下你觉得有趣的事情时，游戏就会改变。你感到被赋予了力量，因为你可以更强大地等待它......学习、记忆和创造。

Famous Examples著名例子

- Eminem made notes. He called it Stacking Ammo. It is a core part of his creative process. Without his notes, Eminem would have remained Marshall Mathers. Are you gonna tell Eminem to stop making notes? I didn't think so.阿姆做了笔记。他称之为堆叠弹药。这是他创作过程的核心部分。如果没有他的笔记，阿姆将仍然是马歇尔·马瑟斯。你要告诉阿姆停止做笔记吗？我不这么认为。
- Are you going to admonish Plato and Aristotle for writing down their thoughts? Maybe we should be saying thanks instead.你要告诫柏拉图和亚里士多德写下他们的想法吗？也许我们应该说声谢谢。

Rebellion叛乱

- Taking notes, journaling, and making notes: it's an act of rebellion in the modern world. When the world says "if you didn't ship, it doesn't count" the note-maker remembers who wins the race between the tortoise and the hare. And the last time I read that story, the ending didn't change. All that glitters isn't gold, and moving slow beats moving fast, when moving fast means moving in circles. 做笔记、写日记和做笔记：这是现代世界的一种反叛行为。当世界说“如果你不发货，那就不算数”时，笔记制作者会记得谁赢得了和野兔之间的比赛。而我上一次读到那个故事，结局并没有改变。闪闪发光的不是金子，缓慢移动比快速移动，当快速移动意味着绕圈移动时。
